The Story

Our cookies are crafted in small batches using the finest local ingredients — Emirati dates, Iranian saffron, Lebanese pistachios, and the beloved kataifi pastry found throughout the souks of Deira.

Each recipe honours the vibrant culinary heritage of Dubai, reinterpreted through the lens of modern artisan baking. No preservatives. No shortcuts. Just pure, golden flavour.
